Welcome to Chelsea FC

Chelsea Football Club is an English professional football club based in Fulham, London. Founded in 1905, the club competes in the Premier League, the top division of English football.

About Chelsea FC

The club has won numerous trophies, including the Premier League, FA Cup, and UEFA Champions League. Chelsea's home ground is Stamford Bridge, which has a capacity of 40,834.
